# Privacy Policy — BhashaLoop

**Last updated: April 2026**

BhashaLoop ("we", "our", or "us") is a Bengali language learning app. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Account information (optional)
If you choose to create an account, we collect:
- Email address
- Display name
- Password (stored as a secure hash — we never see your plain-text password)
- If you sign in with Google or Facebook: your name and email as shared by those services

You can use BhashaLoop without creating an account. Anonymous users get a temporary ID to save progress locally.

### Learning progress
We store your in-app progress including:
- XP (experience points) and daily XP
- Lesson and unit completion history
- Current streak and last active date
- Hearts remaining
- Coins and Bazaar item activations

This data is stored on your device (AsyncStorage) and, if you're signed in, in Firebase (Google's cloud database).

### Usage analytics
We record anonymised events such as lesson starts, lesson completions, screen views, and exercise results. These are stored in our Firebase project and are used solely to improve the app. We do not share this data with advertising networks.

### Purchase information
In-app purchases are processed by Apple (App Store) and RevenueCat. We receive confirmation of successful purchases but do not store your payment card details — those remain with Apple.

### Notifications
If you grant permission, we store a notification token on your device to send local streak reminders. These reminders are scheduled on-device and do not pass through our servers.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- To save and sync your learning progress across devices
- To maintain your streak, XP, and leaderboard ranking
- To deliver streak reminder notifications (only if you grant permission)
- To process Bazaar purchases
- To improve the app based on aggregated usage patterns

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your data for advertising.

## 4. Data Retention

- Anonymous accounts: progress stored locally until you uninstall the app
- Signed-in accounts: your data is kept until you request deletion
- To delete your account and all associated data, email us at **privacy@bhashaloop.com**

---

## 5. Children's Privacy

BhashaLoop is rated 4+ and designed for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

---

## 6. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:
- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Request correction or deletion of your data
- Withdraw consent for data processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at **privacy@bhashaloop.com**.

## 7. Security

We use industry-standard security practices including encrypted transit (HTTPS/TLS) and Firebase's built-in access controls. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect your information.
