## Description (max 4000 chars)

BhashaLoop teaches you Bengali (Bangla) the way it's actually spoken — not just the textbook version, but the real regional dialects that 300 million people use every day.

Whether you grew up hearing Sylheti at home, want to connect with family in Barisal, or are learning Bengali from scratch, BhashaLoop meets you where you are.

**6 DIALECTS. ONE APP.**
• Standard Bengali — শুদ্ধ বাংলা (Bangladesh & West Bengal)
• Sylheti — সিলটি (Sylhet & the UK diaspora)
• Barisali — বরিশালি (Barisal Division)
• Chittagonian — চাটগাঁইয়া (Chittagong Division)
• Rajshahi — রাজশাহী (Rajshahi Division)
• Khulna — খুলনা (Khulna Division)

Each dialect has 12 full units covering Greetings, Introductions, Numbers, Daily Life, Food & Eating, Family & Home, Travel & Directions, Shopping & Money, Work & Daily Life, and Emotions & Socialising.

**HOW IT WORKS**
BhashaLoop uses proven spaced-repetition techniques across six interactive exercise types:

→ Flashcards — build vocabulary with Bengali script + romanisation
→ Multiple choice — test your understanding in context
→ Fill in the blank — learn grammar through natural sentences
→ Match pairs — connect Bengali words to their meanings
→ Translate — go from English to Bengali and back
→ Letter trace — master the Bengali alphabet character by character

**STAY MOTIVATED**
• Daily streaks — build a habit and watch your streak grow
• XP system — earn experience points for every correct answer
• Hearts — stay focused; wrong answers cost a heart
• Leaderboard — see how you rank against other learners
• Streak freeze & XP boosts — pick up power-ups in the Bazaar

**LEARN AT YOUR OWN PACE**
Five minutes a day is enough. Pick up where you left off on any device — your progress syncs automatically.

**WHO IS THIS FOR?**
• Bengali diaspora reconnecting with their heritage language
• Anyone learning Bengali for travel, work, or love
• Students who want a structured, gamified learning path
• Parents raising bilingual children

Start your first lesson for free. No Bengali knowledge required.
